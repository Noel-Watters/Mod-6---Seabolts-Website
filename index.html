Rhododendron Festival

The Rhododendron Festival, held annually in June at Roan Mountain State Park, celebrates the stunning natural beauty of the region. Featuring over 100 arts and crafts vendors, delicious food, traditional music, and old-time demonstrations, it's a two-day celebration that honors the breathtaking rhododendron gardens atop Roan Mountain. Enjoy local culture, outdoor activities, and family-friendly fun, all while supporting community projects.
